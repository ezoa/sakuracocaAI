The Cocoa Industry Challenge

Lack of Transparency

Current systems make it impossible to verify cocoa origins and quality claims, leading to distrust in the supply chain.

Farmer Underpayment

Farmers receive only 6-7% of chocolate's retail price, trapped in cycles of poverty despite producing a luxury good.

Compliance Risks

New EU deforestation laws require proof of sustainable sourcing, creating urgent need for verifiable traceability.

Our AI-Powered Solution

How CacaoScope AI Works

1. Farmer Registration

Farmers are onboarded with biometric verification, creating tamper-proof digital identities.

2. Quality Assessment

Our mobile app uses AI to grade cocoa bean quality through image recognition and sensor data.

3. Secure Tracking

Each batch receives a unique QR code linked to blockchain for immutable tracking through the supply chain.

4. Data Insights

Cooperatives and brands access real-time dashboards with quality metrics and sustainability analytics.

Key Features

Our comprehensive solution combines multiple technologies to create an end-to-end traceability system.

AI Grading
